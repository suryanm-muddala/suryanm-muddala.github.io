About

Suryanarayana Murthy Muddala working as a Researcher in Computer Vision and Machine Learning at Cipherstone Technologies.

Previously, he worked in Computer vision at Facebook connectivity Lab. His research interests include Computer Vision, Machine Learning, 3D and Plenoptics. He lead Street Address for all group, who worked with a unique address (linear, hierarchical and memorable) for each location in the world.
